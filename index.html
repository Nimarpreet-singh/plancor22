Our Mission

At Plancor, we're on a mission to transform agricultural waste into valuable, sustainable products while supporting farming communities.

Founded in 2023, we work directly with farmers to collect and repurpose agricultural waste materials that would otherwise be burned or sent to landfills. Through innovative processing techniques, we create high-quality, eco-friendly products that help reduce environmental impact.

Our commitment to sustainability extends beyond our products - we ensure fair compensation for farmers, use minimal packaging, and continuously improve our manufacturing processes.
